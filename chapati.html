Grilled Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken thighs (boneless or boneless)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey or brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Jalapeño, sliced (for garnish)
  • Optional: chili flakes for extra heat

Preparation:

  1. Marinate: Mix soy sauce, honey, lime juice, garlic powder, smoked paprika, pepper, salt, and olive oil. Coat the chicken thighs and let them marinate 30 min – 2 hrs in the fridge
  2. Grill: Preheat grill to medium‑high heat. Grill chicken 5–7 min per side, until internal temp reaches 75°C (165°F) and you get nice charred marks
  3. Rest: Remove chicken from grill, let rest 5 min.
  4. ⁠Serve: Arrange on a plate, sprinkle chopped cilantro, and top with jalapeño slices for freshness and heat.
