Ninho Milk Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Heaping Tablespooms of Butter
  • Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 2 Cups of Wheat Flour
  • 1/2 Cup of Ninho Milk Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon of Baking Powder
  • Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract

Preparation:

  1. Crack 3 eggs in a bowl and beat
  2. Add sugar until mixture is homogenous
  3. Add Butter at room temperature (half melted), mix well
  4. Add milk powder, and weat slowly
  5. Add and lightly mix baking powder
  6. Add batther to the cake pan which should already be buttered and flowered
  7. Bake for 45 minutes at 180°C (350°F)

Cake ready, frost to your liking, you can make a ganache or just a frosting with condensed milk and decorate with some fruit.
